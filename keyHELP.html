Room Key Help


Need help on how to use/get hold of your room key? Please read the documentation below:
  1. First, download the Worst Eastern(tm) app by scanning the QR code below:*

  2. Open Navigation Drawer, & tap on Room Key.

  3. Enter your login details. Once done, your digital room key will appear on the main app page, with the hotel name, location, and your room number

  4. To get into your room, open the Worst Eastern(tm) app, and scan your smartphone against the reader** (key must have been opened first!!)

*Compatible with smartphones running Android 7 or above
**Scanning function only available on smartphones with NFC
